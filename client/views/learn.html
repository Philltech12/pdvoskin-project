Hello and welcome to my game that I had to design for my Software Development II class called the Oregon Trail. My game is based off of a real game called the Oregon Trail which is a lot more complex and professional.

In my game you start off as a party of 5 members, where one of the member's is the wagon leader and you need to travel the Oregon Trail. You have 45 days to complete this journey or else you lose.

Throughout your trip you will run in a variety of different terrains and each terrain will have its own weather. The desert for example will mostly have really hot weather, whereas the tundra terrain will have mostly cold weathers. There will be a display bar on the bottom as well as a wagon moving across the screen for reference on how much longer you have to go. Make it across the page and you become an Oregon Trail Champion.

Good luck and may the odds be ever in your favor! Go and become an Oregon Trail Champion!
